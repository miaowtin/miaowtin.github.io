Homotopy-coherent mathematics


This is a seminar on homotopy-coherent mathematics I organised at Jagiellonian University together with Rafał Mach. It takes place each Friday at 16:15 in room 0122, Loj6.
  1. (29.11) Rafał Mach, An introduction to nLab. This is a short introduction to the language of adjoint functors and model categories.
  2. (06.12) Martin Leshko, Cohesive topoi, I. The goal of this talk is threefold: the first one is to introduce the classical theory of Grothendieck topoi briefly but concisely; the second is to discuss the formalism and philosophy of modalities and then conclude with the notion of cohesive topoi as they are crucial for our highly abstract formulations of the principles of differential topology and geometry.
